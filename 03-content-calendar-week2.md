```
- Your EA needs fast execution
- Look for < 50ms average execution time
- ECN/STP brokers are best

- Scalping EAs need ultra-low spreads
- Compare spreads during different sessions
- Raw spread accounts > standard accounts

- Always confirm before depositing
- Look for "algo trading friendly" in their features

5. REGULATION
```

### Post 1 (Morning) — EDUCATIONAL
```
The #1 reason trading bots fail (it's not what you think):

It's not bad code.
It's not the strategy.
It's not the broker.

It's OVERFITTING.

What's overfitting?
When an EA is optimized TOO perfectly for past data, it fails in live markets.

It's like studying the exact answers to last year's exam — then the questions change.

How we prevent it:
1. Out-of-sample testing (test on data the EA has never seen)
2. Walk-forward optimization
3. Monte Carlo simulation
4. Multiple pair/timeframe testing
5. Conservative parameter selection

This is why cheap, mass-produced bots fail. They're overfit to look good in backtests.

Our EAs are built to survive real markets. That's the difference.

#overfitting #algotrading #quantitative #tradingbot #education
```
