## QUICK REFERENCE: DAILY ROUTINE

| Time (IST) | Task | Duration |
|-------------|------|----------|
| 8:00-8:30 | Check overnight DMs, respond | 30 min |
| 8:30-9:00 | Publish morning post | 30 min |
| 9:00-9:45 | Send 25 connection requests | 45 min |
| 9:45-10:30 | Engage: 15 comments on target audience posts | 45 min |
| 2:00-2:30 | Publish afternoon post | 30 min |
| 2:30-3:15 | Send 25 connection requests + follow-up DMs | 45 min |
| 3:15-3:45 | Engage: 15 comments | 30 min |
| 7:00-7:30 | Publish evening post | 30 min |
| 7:30-8:00 | Respond to all DMs + comments | 30 min |
| 8:00-8:30 | Update daily tracker | 30 min |

**Total daily time: ~5.5 hours**
