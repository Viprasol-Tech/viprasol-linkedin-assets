### Guide Content (short version to DM):
```
5 MISTAKES THAT KILL 90% OF TRADING BOTS

Mistake 1: OVERFITTING
Your EA looks amazing in backtesting but fails live. Why? It's been optimized for past data too perfectly.
Fix: Always do out-of-sample testing. If backtest shows 95% win rate, it's probably overfit.

Mistake 2: NO RISK MANAGEMENT
No stop loss, no position limits, no daily loss cap = guaranteed account blow.
Fix: Maximum 2% risk per trade. Max 3 open positions. Daily loss limit of -5%.

Mistake 3: WRONG BROKER
High spreads, slow execution, or EA-unfriendly policies kill profitability.
Fix: Use ECN/STP brokers with < 50ms execution. Always confirm they allow EAs.

Mistake 4: NO VPS
Running EA on your laptop = stops when you sleep, disconnect, or restart.
Fix: Use a VPS ($10-30/month) near your broker's server. Run 24/7.

Mistake 5: PANIC TURNING OFF
You turn off the EA after 3 consecutive losses. The next 5 trades would have been winners.
Fix: Trust the backtest. Set drawdown limits in the code. Review weekly, not hourly.

-- Viprasol Tech | viprasol.com
Want a custom EA? DM me.
```
